How it works

Three functional layers.

Pump Bar is built around the pre-workout window: fast energy first, then nitric oxide-supporting ingredients for the pump window later.

01

Pink Manuka™ core

The signature centre: a thick pink Manuka honey blend with beetroot and pomegranate.

Manuka honey

Natural carbohydrates for fast pre-workout energy.

Beetroot

Rich in dietary nitrates, which support nitric oxide production and blood flow.

Pomegranate

Polyphenol-rich fruit linked with antioxidant support and vascular function.

02

80% dark chocolate shell

Rich dark chocolate provides cocoa flavanols, which are associated with vascular health and blood flow. It also gives Pump Bar its premium taste and makes it feel like real food, not another powder.

03

Celtic sea salt flakes

Salt provides sodium, an important electrolyte lost through sweat. It supports hydration, balances the sweetness, and gives the bar its visible premium finish.
